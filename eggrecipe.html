Scrambled Eggs

Here is a recipe for scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted and cream the eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When eggs are still moist remove from heat (it will continue to cook on plate until served).
